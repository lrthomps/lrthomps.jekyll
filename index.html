Books!

I try to review everything I read. I fail on some comics, some short stories, but those don't count to the number of books read...

Lara's bookshelf: read

Thoughts on Design
by Paul Rand
An essay compiled with many examples as a little book. A lot of theory, most of it with application. Better aimed at someone already with a background in design but still a lot to appreciate for the layman.
300 Fox Way Holiday Piece
by Maggie Stiefvater
A Minor Raven Boys Holiday Drabble
by Maggie Stiefvater
The Art of Perspective: Who Tells the Story
by Christopher Castellani
Very interesting book on the point of view (POV), narrative structure, and other perspective related writing concerns. If you liked The Art of Subtext: Beyond Plot, then this book of the same series is a must read. Highly recommend.
